# NEURA for Microsoft 365 (planned)

**Status:** planned / design. Not released.

## Idea

An assistant that shows up inside **Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook**,
in the place users expect Copilot, but backed by **your Open WebUI**
(models, RAG, tools, policies).

Document generation reuses the same engines as the Open WebUI tools:

- Word → Generate Documents
- PowerPoint → Generate Slides
- Excel → Generate Spreadsheets

## Why it belongs in NEURA Office

Open WebUI tools prove the generation quality in chat.
The MS365 add-in brings that quality **into the apps people already use**,
without sending company data to a third-party Copilot stack.
